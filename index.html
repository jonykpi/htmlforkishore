Bayanat Around the World

During Expo 2020, Bayanat announced its sponsorship of Cmdr. (Retd.) Abhilash Tomy’s participation in the Golden Globe Race 2022.

Abhilash will sail around the world in a UAE-registered boat named ‘Bayanat’ with race number 71 — a tribute to 1971 — the UAE’s year of formation.

About the Golden Globe Race

Considered the world’s toughest and longest sailboat race, the Golden Globe Race 2022 (GGR 2022) is a grueling nonstop circumnavigation of the world, where participants sail alone without the use of any modern technology and armed only with sextants and paper charts.

The race spans approximately 48,000 kilometers and begins on September 4, 2022 from Les Sables-d’Olonne, located on the western coast of France.

Who is Abhilash Tomy?

Abhilash is a highly decorated Indian Naval Officer who in 2012 became the first Indian and seventy-ninth person in history to complete a solo, non-stop circumnavigation of the world by sea. To date, Abhilash has completed over 100,000 kilometers of sailing.

As a Golden Globe Race 2018 participant, after 82 days, while in third position, Abhilash's boat was damaged in a storm, during which he also suffered life threatening injuries with a broken spine. He is now even more determined to complete what he started in 2018.

In February 2022, Abhilash visited the Bayanat office and spoke about his naval experience, the challenges he faced in 2018 and his mindset as he prepares for the GGR 2022
